Research & Insights

At BlackBox RC, we explore the intersection of financial science, and astificial intelligence. Our research focuses on understansing market inefficencies, investor sentiment, and predictive modeling machine learning and natural processing.

Current Focus Areas

1. AI in Financial Forecasting
LLMs like ChatGPT and fine-tuned BERT variants to analyze financial news, earning calls, and social media for sentiment signals. Our model test whether AI-generated sentiment can predict short-term stock returns and volatility.

2. Behaviroal Biases in Asset Pricing
We invevstigate how cognitive biases - such as overconfidence, herding, and loss aversion - influence pricing animalies. By combining survey data with market behavior, we build models that netter reflect real-world decision-making.

3. Machine Learning for Corporate Finance
From capital structure to M&A decisions, we apply supervised learning to identify patterns in corporate behavior. Our work helps predict financial distress, dividend changes, and investment usign both traditional and alternative data.
